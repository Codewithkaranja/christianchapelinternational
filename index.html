Discover the story and mission of Masii Christian Chapel

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Masii Christian Chapel began as a small prayer group meeting in a local home. Through God's grace and the dedication of our members, we've grown into a vibrant community of faith serving Masii and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, discipleship, and service. We believe in transforming lives through the power of the Gospel and making a positive impact in our community.

We are an interdenominational church welcoming believers from all backgrounds to worship together in unity and love.
