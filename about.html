Our Vision

At Christian Chapels International, we are committed to nurturing thriving churches in villages and cities around the world. Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, discipleship, and service.

We believe in transforming lives through the power of the Gospel and making a positive impact in our community. Through our various ministries and outreach programs, we strive to be a beacon of hope and love in Masii and beyond.

Our Story

The story of CCI is not without the people of Masii, South Eastern Kenya and the founders, Stanley and Rose, who also co-founded Tumaini International.

"Why don't you start a Church for us?"

Around 2006, while Stanley and Rose were busy with ministry work through Tumaini, people from Masii approached them and asked, "Why don't you start a Church for us?".

It so happened that the people yearned for a Church where they could worship without being judged or victimized. Stanley and Rose were hesitant but later felt the Lord calling them to plant a community Chapel in Masii town. They put together a team to pray and discern the next steps, and in the process, Stanley was connected to a young college graduate from Scott Christian University in Machakos, William Maweu, who became the founding pastor of the first church plant- Christian Chapels International in late 2008.

The Mission of the Church was simple- Knowing Christ and making Him known through the word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit.
